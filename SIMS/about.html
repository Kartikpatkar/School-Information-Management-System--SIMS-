Campus, its Location: Priority Highlands, the beautiful 100-acre campus, situated in Chanthar Village in Brahmavar, Udupi District is the home of LRIS. This campus is popularly known as Alpine Convent School, and is 4 kms east of Brahmavar Bus Station on the Brahmavar-Hebri Road. Brahmavar is on National Highway No. 66, about 72 kms north of Mangalore.
The nearest Airport is at Mangalore. The nearest Railway Station is in Udupi, about 20 kms from the Campus. Brahmavar lies between Udupi in the south (12 kms) and Kundapura in the north (24 kms), on NH 66.
